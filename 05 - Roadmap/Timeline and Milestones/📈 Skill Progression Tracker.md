

## Skill Assessment Framework

### Skill Level Definitions

```markdown
1-2: Novice (Heard of it, basic awareness)
3-4: Beginner (Can follow tutorials, basic usage)
5-6: Intermediate (Can build projects, understands patterns)
7-8: Advanced (Can solve complex problems, teach others)
9-10: Expert (Can architect solutions, contribute to community)
```
